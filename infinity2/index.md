# infinity2

# infinity2m

## Specs

- Dual Cortex A7
- Only 64KB of internal SRAM
- 16KB boot ROM
- Boot from SPI NOR or SPI NAND

## SSD201/SSD202D

Seems to be all of the base stuff the other chips have with an extra ethernet
controller, a video decoder intead of encoder etc.

- Chip id: 0xf0
